About Us




We are a group of six students studying at the University of Wollongong, and we’ve banded together to make “Cultural Pentagon”. Our team is made of both Computer Science and Information Technology students.

Yes, there’s six of us, but we have chosen to be Cultural Pentagon as we come from five different cultures – Japan, Vietnam, China, Philippines and Australia. Our logo consists of five triangles arranged in the shape of a pentagon, with each having an arrow pointing towards the centre – representing how we have come together from different parts of the world.
