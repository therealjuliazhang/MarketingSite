About Us

We are a group of six stduents studying at the University if Wollongong that has banded together to make "Cultural Pentagon". Our name "Cultural Pentagon" may be confusing as there are six of us, however our name shows the five different cultures (Japan, Vietnam, China, Phillippines, Australia) that we represent. Our logo is a wave in a pentagon, which represents out group name. The pentagon refers to the pentagon part of our name, however the wave represents the sea or the letter "C" representing the cultural part in our name - in other words, it's a bad pun.
