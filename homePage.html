About Us

We are a group of six students studying at the University if Wollongong that has banded together to make "Cultural Pentagon". Our name "Cultural Pentagon" may be confusing as there are six of us, however our name shows the five different cultures (Japan, Vietnam, China, Phillippines, Australia) that we represent. Our logo is a wave in a pentagon, which represents our group name. The pentagon refers to the pentagon part of our name, however the wave represents the sea or the letter "C" representing the cultural part in our name - in other words, it's a bad pun.

Our Philosophy

Think Big, Start Small

Here at Cultural Pentagon we like to think big and consider all possibilities upcoming in the future and put time into our plans in order to understand emerging context to avoid assuming our current assumption are correct.

Think small - rather then just jumping straight into the deep end of the pool we break out ideas into smaller parts. This allows us to focus our ventures in smaller parts while linking them to the bigger picture.
